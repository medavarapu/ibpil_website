Technical Education

IBEUL has executed MoU with Govt. of Odisha on 24.05.2010 for the establishment of one ITI near our plant. For this venture, the company has taken a land of 3.20 acres at its own cost. We have supplemented the giant efforts of the Hon’ble CM in establishing ITI in the trades of Electrician, Fitter & Welder at Kumbharbandh in Lakhanpur Tahasil of Jharsuguda District for providing vocational training to the local youth for free of cost. The classes have been started from Sept. 2012 with 56 students. The deprived youth of the locality are being given skilled training to enable them to work in industries. The total expenditure for setting up this Institution is Rs 3.25 crores. Prior to ITI, we have provided multi skilled training to number of local unemployed youth through Tata Refractories Limited.

Dispensary

Health is wealth. Keeping this in view we have set up dispensaries at all our plant sites. MBBS doctors along with supporting staff have been appointed in the dispensaries. The company has been creating health awareness among the local people by organizing campaigns for treatment of dengue / blood donation camps.
