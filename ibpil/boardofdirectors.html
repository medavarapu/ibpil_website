K Raghu Ramakrishnam Raju

Chairman and Managing Director

Mr. K Raghu Ramakrishnam Raju is the Chairman and Managing Director of the Ind Barath group. He is a post graduate in Pharmacy from Andhra University. With a view to contribute in bridging the huge gap between demand and supply of power in the country, Mr. Raghu has put all his efforts for the cause. In line with the same, he has promoted Ind Barath Power Infra limited. Mr. Raghu has over 20 years of experience in commissioning, operating and managing power projects. He is actively involved in the day to day operations, including marketing and financial functions of the group.

Under his leadership, the group has already set up power plants. Starting with a small 6 MW biomass plant, the group has expanded in to Hydel, Natural Gas, Coal and wind with a total capacity of 3578 MW. Mr. Raghu has led the commissioning of the first biomass plant in the state of Andhra Pradesh. Again for the first time, he has successfully commissioned the gas based power plant in the state in private sector in association with the Caterpillar USA, the world leaders in the gas engines. Both were the companies privileged for the first time to have third party sale of power and were commissioned in the shortest gestation period. He was also the co-promoter of two gas based plants, Arkay Energy Limited and RVK Energy Limited.

The group under the leadership of Mr. Raghu today is present in Andhra Pradesh, Chattisgarh, Himachal Pradesh, Kerala, Maharashtra, Orissa and Tamil Nadu.

Mr. Raghu is the recipient of “Vijaya Shree Award” from India International Friendship Society for enriching human life and outstanding attainments. He received “Young Entrepreneurship Award” for his spirited enterprise and commitment to the spread of renewable energy from Solar Society of India.

Mr. Raghu currently holds positions such as President of the Bio-Mass Power Producers Association Tamilnadu, President of the Maharashtra Bio-Mass Energy Developers Association. In the past, he was also the President of the Bio-mass Developers Association of Andhra Pradesh.
