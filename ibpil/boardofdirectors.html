K Raghu Ramakrishnam Raju

Chairman and Managing Director

Mr. K Raghu Ramakrishnam Raju is the Chairman and Managing Director of our Group. He is a post graduate from Andhra University. Mr. Raghu has over 20 years of experience in commissioning, operating and managing power projects. He is actively involved in the day to day operations, including marketing and financial functions of the Group.

In the last two decades, Mr. Raghu has commissioned 12 power projects. He started the first power project using rice husk as fuel in 1998. He has led the commissioning of the first Biomass and Gas based power plants in private sector in the state of Andhra Pradesh. Both were the companies privileged for the first time to have third party sale of power and were commissioned in shortest gestation period. He was also the co-promoter of two gas based plants, RVK Energy Limited and Arkay Energy Limited.

He currently holds positions such as the President of the Bio Mass Power Producers Association Tamil Nadu, President of the Maharashtra Bio-Mass Energy Developers Association. In the past he has also been the President of the Biomass Developers Association of Andhra Pradesh.
