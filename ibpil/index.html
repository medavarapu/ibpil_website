About Us

IND-BARATH POWER INFRA LIMITED (IBPIL) is a successful and long standing power infrastructure development company with a clear focus on development of power projects, based on variety of fuels spread across the country. IBPIL is the holding company, providing funds to its SPV subsidiaries for development. The group has successfully passed through various phases of the industry, before and after the Electricity Act 2003. Under the able leadership of Mr. K Raghu, a dynamic industrialist, IBPIL has witnessed phenomenal growth and is set to become a leading player in the Indian power sector.
